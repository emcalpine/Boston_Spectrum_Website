The Boston Spectrum

The Boston Spectrum Banquet Facility of Elizabeth Township

The Boston Spectrum is a multi-purpose banquet hall owned by the Elizabeth Township Fire Department. Our mission is to make the Boston Spectrum available to be part of a memory you will not forget!

General Information

Catering:

Details available from the Banquet Hall Coordinator.

Bar / Lounge:

Fully equipped wet bar with 15' serving area.

Amenities:

Climate controlled, including HERPA air filtration.

Filtered water in soda, ice machines, and at the bar.

Handicap-acessible rest rooms on same level as hall.

Ample on-site parking including handicapped spaces.
