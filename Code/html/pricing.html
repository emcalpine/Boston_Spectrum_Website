Pricing

Rental Deposit - $400 ($200 upon contract agreement - $200 within 6 weeks)

Balance of Hall Rental must be paid 90 days prior to the Event Date (Excluding Soda & Bar charges).

All rental deposits are non-refundable

Banquet Hall Rental Prices - All prices are for an 8 hour rental - additional hours can be negotiated with the Hall Coordinator.

Booking Rates

  • Saturday: $1000

  • Friday: $750

  • Sunday thru Thursday: $350

( Wedding Reception and Organizational prices are not inclusive.)

What's included in this rental fee:

  • Set up / Tear down
  • Clean up
  • On-site manager

Soda / Bar Charges (5 hours – additional hours can be negotiated with the Hall Coordinator)

$2.50 per person – Additional charges for Bartender(s). Bartenders must be used if alcoholic beverages or the soda system is used at the event.

What is included in this charge:

  • Cups
  • Ice
  • Mixers
  • Soda
  • Juices (oranges and cranberry)
  • Napkins
  • Stirrers
  • Garnishes (lemons limes, and cherries)

***All alcoholic beverages must be supplied by the renter***

Approved Cateres for the Boston Spectrum Banquet Facility:

  • Woody’s (Versailles) – 412-754-0551
  • Carriage Inn (Eliz. Twp.) – 412-754-1221
  • Stratwood (White Oak) – 412-672-9659
  • Twin Oaks Catering (White Oak) - 412-678-3321
  • Food-A-Rama (Madison) 724-446-9398

(Outside caterers may be negotiated with the Spectrum Coordinator – additional fees will apply)

Acceptable forms of payment are Cash, Check, Master Card, Visa, Discover or Money Order. All forms of payment must be to “Greenock Volunteer Fire Company”.
