Pricing

Rental Deposit - $200 (This holds the date and is used as a security deposit)

Balance of Hall Rental must be paid 90 days prior to the Event Date (Excluding Soda & Bar charges).

Rental Deposits may be returned after rental and upon hall inspection.

Banquet Hall Rental Prices - All prices are for an 8 hour rental - additional hours can be negotiated with the Hall Coordinator.

Booking Rates

  • Saturday: $1000

  • Friday & Sunday: $750

  • Monday through Thursday: $400

( Wedding Reception and Organizational prices are not inclusive.)

What's included in this rental fee:

  • Usage of the main hall
  • Clean up
  • On-site manager

Soda / Bar Charges (8 hours – additional hours can be negotiated with the Hall Manager)

$2.50 per person up to $800 – Additional charge of $75 per Bartender. Bartenders must be used if alcoholic beverages or the soda system is used at the event.

What is included in this charge:

  • Cups
  • Ice
  • Mixers
  • Soda
  • Juices (oranges and cranberry)
  • Napkins
  • Stirrers
  • Garnishes (lemons limes, and cherries)

***All alcoholic beverages must be supplied by the lessee***

Approved Cateres for the Boston Spectrum Banquet Facility:

  • Woody’s (Versailles) – 412-754-0551
  • Carriage Inn (Eliz. Twp.) – 412-754-1221
  • Stratwood (White Oak) – 412-672-9659
  • Twin Oaks Catering (White Oak) - 412-678-3321
  • Food-A-Rama (Madison) 724-446-9398

(Outside caterers may be negotiated with the Spectrum Manager – additional fees will apply)

Acceptable forms of payment are Cash, Check, or Money Order. All forms of payment must be to “Greenock Volunteer Fire Company”.
