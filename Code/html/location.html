Location

The Boston Spectrum is located at 6001 Smithfield St, Boston, 15135

From Route 51

Follow Route 48 North approximately 7 miles to the Boston Bridge. Make a right before the bridge on to East Smithfield Street. Proceed approx. 0.2 miles. The Boston Spectrum Banquet Hall will be on your left.

From Route 30

From Route 30 follow Route 48 South approximately 6.3 miles to the Boston Bridge. Make a left at the end of the bridge on to East Smithfield Street. Proceed approx. 0.2 mile. The Boston Spectrum Banquet Hall will be on your left.
